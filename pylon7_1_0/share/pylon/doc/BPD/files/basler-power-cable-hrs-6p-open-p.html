Basler Power Cable, HRS 6p/open, P#

Power cable with HRS 6-pin connector and open end.

Info

The cable is suitable for the following camera models:

  • aviator CL
  • racer
Order Number Cable Length
2000029758 10 m

Cable Drawing#

CAUTION – Personal Injury Hazard

Personal injury hazard and risk of damage to camera in case of short circuits (applies to GigE cameras only)

Short circuits may cause an extreme rise in temperature of the camera's housing. This may damage the camera and may also lead to personal injuries, e.g., burns if touching the housing. In the worst case, the overheating may cause a fire. In order to prevent that, you should take additional measures to limit the current flowing through each individual wire during a short circuit. The maximum current allowed is 2 A. There are two ways how to do this:

  • Using a fuse
  • Using a limited power supply
