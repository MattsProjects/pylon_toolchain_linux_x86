marathon ACX-SP#

marathon ACX-SP is a one-channel CoaXPress frame grabber supporting the CoaXPress standard in version 1.1.

One CXP DIN connector is located directly on the slot bracket.

For connection to the computer, marathon ACX-SP is plugged into a PCIe 2.x (Gen 2) x 4 slot of your computer.
